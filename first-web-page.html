Graduate Student Association

Official site for CSGSB

The Computer Science Graduate Student Association (CSGSA or GSA) provides graduate students with social, academic, and professional opportunities to improve their career at Purdue and afterwards. The elected Computer Science Graduate Student Board (CSGSB or GSB) serves as the leadership of the GSA, and acts as a liaison between the students and department/university leadership.

Joining GSA

All Purdue CS graduate students have automatic membership in the GSA.

Joining GSB

Membership in the GSB is open to any member of the GSA. The GSA will have elections for its new GSB officers at the end of the spring semester each year. Please see the GSA constitution on this page for further details.
